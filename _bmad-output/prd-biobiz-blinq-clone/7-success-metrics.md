# 7. Success Metrics

> *Target values are placeholders — validate with stakeholders before finalizing.*

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| User activation rate | % of signups who complete onboarding and create first card | ≥ 80% |
| Card share rate | Avg shares per active user per week | ≥ 2 |
| Contact conversion rate | % of card views that result in a mutual contact exchange | ≥ 15% |
| Premium conversion rate | % of free users who convert to premium within 30 days | ≥ 5% |
| DAU/MAU ratio | Daily active users / Monthly active users | ≥ 0.25 |
| Day-7 retention | % of new users returning 7 days after signup | ≥ 40% |
| Day-30 retention | % of new users returning 30 days after signup | ≥ 20% |
| QR scan success rate | % of QR scans that successfully load the card page | ≥ 99% |
