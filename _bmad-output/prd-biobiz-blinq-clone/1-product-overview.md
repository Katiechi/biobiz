# 1. Product Overview

**BioBiz** is a digital business card platform that allows professionals to create, customize, and share digital business cards instantly. Users can exchange contact information via QR codes, NFC, links, and multiple messaging channels. The platform includes contact scanning, AI-powered meeting notes, and a contact management system.

**Target Users:** Professionals, freelancers, entrepreneurs, sales teams, and anyone who networks regularly.

**Platforms:** Mobile (Android + iOS), Web (card viewer/landing page)
