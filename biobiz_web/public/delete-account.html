Delete Your BioBiz Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your BioBiz account and all associated data using any of the methods below.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the BioBiz app
  2. Go to Menu (top-left hamburger icon)
  3. Tap Manage Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Email Us

Send an email to support@biobiz.app from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete My Account". We will process your request within 48 hours.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and account credentials
  • Your digital business card(s)
  • All contacts saved in your account
  • All notes attached to contacts
  • Audio recordings and AI transcriptions
  • Analytics data (card views, shares, scans)

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data Retention

After account deletion, all personal data is removed from our systems within 30 days. Some anonymized, non-personal data may be retained for analytics purposes.

For questions, contact us at support@biobiz.app
